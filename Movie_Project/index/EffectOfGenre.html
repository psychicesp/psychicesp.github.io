Effect of Movie Content Rating

The busy monstrosity above shows whether or not a movie being in a specific genre will impact its IMDb Rating. There are much larger factors in the quality of a movie than its classification, but as you can see there are a few large differences that stick out.

An ANOVA resulting in an extreemely significant P value < 10-100 showed that at least one of these groups is significantly different. This was somewhat surprising because many most movies are essentially copied in multiple categories which should pull all of their means together. Given more time I would have run a multi-variate AVOVA followed by a Tukey test which would be a much more appropriate series of tests.
